The CSSA holds open office hours where you can come talk to us about any of the following:

  • Recommendations/student perspective on classes and scheduling

  • Help with courses (if we can!)

  • General questions about the student experience in the major

  • Questions about undergraduate research

  • Questions about life or cognitive science more generally
